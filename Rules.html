Rules for the Programming Class

Respect and Listening

Each student must respect the opinions and ideas of others and listen attentively during discussions."

Punctuality

Arriving on time to class is essential to avoid missing anything and to respect others' time."

Active Participation

Get involved in class activities and ask questions to foster collective learning

Use of Technology

Use electronic devices appropriately and only when permitted

Collaborative Work

Work together with classmates, sharing responsibilities and supporting each other.
